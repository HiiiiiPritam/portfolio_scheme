Check your eligibility for the Scheme
  • Must have a valid Local Residence Certificate of Jharkhand issued by Competent Authority OR Passed Class 10th from any recognized School in Jharkhand, if applying for loan to pursue Diploma Level Programmes OR Passed Class 10th and Class 12th / Diploma from any School / College / College / University in Jharkhand, if applying for loan to pursue Degree Level Programmes.
  • Must not be more that 40 Years of age while applying for the loan.
  • Must not be prior beneficiary of the Scheme.
  • Must have secured admission in any eligible College / University under the Scheme.
Documents and Information for submission of loan application

Keep the following documents and information handy while proceeding to fill the application form:-

  • Basic Information such as Name, Age, Gender and Social Category.
  • Details of academic institution from where the student has passed Class 10th and Class 12th / Diploma.
  • Personal Bank Account Details of the Student.
  • Details of the Academic Institution, Course, Duration and Tution Fees of the Programme.
  • Choice of the Bank, District and Branch Details for seeking loan.
  • Break-up of Institutional and Non-Institutional Amount within the total amount.
  • Mobile Number linked with Aadhaar Number for OTP Certification.
  • The documents required are as follows:-
    • Marksheet and Certificate of Class 10th
    • Marksheet and Certificate of Class 12th
    • Aadhaar Card of Student and Co-applicant
    • Photo and Signature of Student
    • Photo and Signature of Co-applicant (Father / Mother / Legal Guardian)
    • Front Page of Bank Passbook / Cancelled Cheque of Student
    • Front Page of Bank Passbook / Cancelled Cheque of Co-applicant
    • Proof of Admission in the applied Academic Institution
    • Any document mentioning the Bank Account Details of the Student
    • Fee Structure provided by the concerned Educational Institute
    • No Objection Certificate (in case you have an existing loan and have foreclosed it before applying for loan under the Scheme)

With the following details, please proceed for application and submit your application

  • After, application please proceed to the Selected Bank and Branch for processing of loan.
  • In case of any queries related to loan processing, please contact the concerned State or District Nodal Officer of your Bank with the details as mentioned at https://gscc.jharkhand.gov.in/Home/ContactUs
  • The concerned Bank shall carry out its internal approval process for each loan and update the status on the GSCC Portal.
  • If approved, the Bank will initiate process for sanctioning and disbursement.
  • If rejected, the student will have the choice to select another MLI as per his / her choice.
  • In case of any issues while filling the Online Application, please use the Toll Free No. 18005693311 on all Working Days except Government Holidays.
Sanctioning and Disbursement
  • After updating the loan’s approval status of the GSCC Web Portal, the MLI shall submit the Loan Account Number, Institutional Loan Amount, Non-institutional Loan Amount, and Total Loan Amount.
  • On updating the amount, the concerned Bank shall download the Tripartite Loan Agreement (hyperlink a sample of TPA) from the GSCC Web Portal.
  • The concerned Bank signs the TPA, gets it signed by the Student & Co-Applicant and shares the TPA with the Department for Signing.
  • The Department Nodal Officer signs the TPA and shares it with the concerned Bank for uploading on the Web Portal.
  • After uploading of TPA, the Bank disburses Institutional Loan Amount to the concerned Institution and updated the same on the GSCC Web Portal.
  • In case of any queries related to loan processing, please contact the concerned State or District Nodal Officer of your Bank with the details as mentioned at https://gscc.jharkhand.gov.in/Home/ContactUs
Repayment of Loan
  • The applicable simple interest rate for all education loans under GSCC is EBLR of SBI + 1.5% for Male Student, and EBLR of SBI + 1% for Female Student.
  • The student has the facility to either pay interest during the study period or pay principal + accrued interest in the form of EMI after the study period.
  • The end interest rate after provisioning for Subsidy from State Government shall be 4% for students who don’t pay interest during study period and 3% for students who pay interest during study period.

Check Your Eligibility

1.The student should be an Indian national and must have completed, from a recognized school in Jharkhand, Class 10th to pursue diploma and Class 10th and 12th to pursue undergraduate or higher courses.

2.The student should have secured admission in institutions like IITs, IIMs, IIESTs, ISIs, NLUs, AIIMSs, NITs, XLRI, IISCs, BITS, SPA, NID, IIFTs, ICFAI Business School etc within and outside of the state (within India) which are ranked up to 200 in the overall list or ranked up to 100in their respective individual category of NIRF Rankings or have been accredited grade either “A" or above by NAAC.

3.The student should not be aged more than 40 (forty) years at the time of application for loan.

4.The student should not be a prior beneficiary of the Scheme.

5.The student should not have availed education loan from any State Co-operative Banks or Central Co-operative Banks or District Central Co-operative Banks or any Public or Private sector Banks for the course in which he/she is applying for loan under the Scheme.

Guruji Student Credit Card (GSCC) Scheme

An overarching goal of the State Government is to guarantee that every student has the chance to access higher education in top institutions of the Country, regardless of their collateral free full guaranteed education loan up to Rs 15 Lakh. This commitment aims to eliminate any barriers that might prevent students from pursuing further studies due to financial constraints.

The objective of this scheme is to offer financial aid from the State Government to students who have successfully completed their Class 10th education in a recognized school within Jharkhand. This assistance entails nominal interest rates, accompanied by appropriate guarantees to the bank. The support aims to facilitate the pursuit of diploma courses for these students. Additionally, students who have completed their Class 10th and Class 12th education from recognized schools in the state are also eligible for this support, intended to assist them in pursuing undergraduate and higher-level courses.
